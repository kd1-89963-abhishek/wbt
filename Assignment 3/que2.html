About Sunbeam Section

At Sunbeam we believe retaining a competitive edge is imperative for any individual in today's professional world.

Sunbeam has institutionalized critical approaches & learning methods for the same purpose.

Not only have the challenges become more demanding, but also the rewards of staying at the forefront seem to be promising.

In this scenario, technical & personal skills which provide effective solutions is now of critical importance for the long term growth of professionals.

Sunbeam recognizes this need and has committed itself to providing a platform for aspiring professionals focused on an aggressive career growth strategy.

Sunbeam's proven track record in bringing about effective transformations in individuals is backed by a solid understanding of the rapidly changing needs of the industry & the global business scenario.

A strong commitment to the growth of its students and employees is visible in its curriculums for training programs and projects offering value-added services to its clients.

A young and dynamic management team and carefully recruited and trained staff members drive Sunbeam's business within a time established period in a short span of time.

Sunbeam Group has a strong client-orientation and ensures a tailored approach to many establishments focused on aggressive growth strategies.

Since its humble beginnings in the late 60's Sunbeam Group has today, evolved into a multi-technology, multi-location competency center with IT professionals capable of delivering high-end technological training & solutions in diverse modes.

Their expertise in various IT fields has made Sunbeam Group a premium turnkey solution provider.
